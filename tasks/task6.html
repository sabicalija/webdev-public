        # Presentation

        # Description

        The goal of this assignment is to prepare a presentation on a selected topic relevant to your upcoming project.

        # Task

        Prepare a presentation for your colleagues on a specific topic (technology, protocol, or API) of your choice.

        Duration: 10 - 15 min

        Your presentation should address

        • Relevance
          Provide some information on the necessity of this technology.
          What problem does it address?
        • Historical Background/Alternatives
          Explain how the technology evolved.
          What other solutions have been used to address this problem?
        • Explanation/Examples
          Provide some basic examples, either by using short code snippets or by explaining concrete use cases.

        Pay attention to your target group when preparing your presentation. Try to summarize essential facts and provide useful examples, helping your colleagues to familiarize themselves with the topic and try to integrate information that may be relevant for them.
